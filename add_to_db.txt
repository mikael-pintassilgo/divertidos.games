Fail conditions
Fail conditions are the rules or states that determine when a player loses, cannot continue a challenge, or fails to achieve a game objective. They create tension, stakes, and meaningful challenge within gameplay.
<br>
Examples:
<br>Losing all health
<br>Running out of time
<br>Falling into a pit
<br>Being caught by enemies
<br>Destroying an important object
<br>Making irreversible bad decisions
<br>Failing to meet mission objectives
